About VizuLabs

A studio of designers, not a design factory.

Founded in 2021, VizuLabs works with a deliberately small roster of clients at any one time — usually no more than four. Every identity is led end-to-end by the same person from first sketch to final handoff, supported by a small circle of collaborators, rather than passed between departments.

We've built identities for venture-backed startups, family-run manufacturers, and everything between — the common thread is a system built to outlast a single campaign or trend.

Process

Five stages, one system

The order matters — each stage locks in the decisions the next one depends on.

01

Discover

Stakeholder interviews, competitive audits and market positioning — establishing what the brand needs to say, and to whom.

02

Define

Strategy locked into a creative brief: positioning statement, tone of voice, and a visual territory to explore.

03

Design

Concept development across mark, colour, and type — presented in context, never in isolation.

04

Refine

Structured rounds of feedback and revision, stress-testing the system across real-world applications.

05

Deliver

Full asset handoff and a living guideline document, so the system scales long after we step back.
